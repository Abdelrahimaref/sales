    Marketo

    Marketplace like Amazon Template

    Standard License

    Standard License

    • Documentation
    • Tutorials
    • Customize this template
    • Download Sketch or PSD

    Marketo no-code template replicates Amazon's main functionality, bringing buyers and sellers together on your custom marketplace platform. It allows selling both physical and digital goods and the template integrates Stripe for split payments, allowing the platform to facilitate payments between the buyer and seller while owner keeps any commission. Customize your admin dashboard with just the functionality you need to make sure transactions are running smoothly.

    Featurs

    • - Landing Page
    • - Responsive Design
    • - Split payments
    • - Admin dashboard
    • - Buyer and Seller dashboards
    • - Dark Mode
